Evil Hangman

Evil Hangman was the first major game that I created when I learned how to program. Instead of a typical game of hangman, the computer never chooses a word and removes all words that contain the letter you picked until there are no more options. Once there are no more options, the computer will give you a letter and will repeat the process again.
